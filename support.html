Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new job?

Tap the + button on the Jobs screen, fill in the customer and site details, then save. You can add photos, notes, and equipment info from within the job.

How does AI diagnostics work?

Open a job, attach a photo or describe the issue in the notes field, then tap Generate Report. Our AI will analyse the information and produce a diagnostic report with recommendations.

Can I use the app offline?

Some features are available offline, but AI report generation and cloud sync require an internet connection. Data entered offline will sync automatically when you reconnect.

How do I sign in with Google?

On the login screen, tap Sign in with Google and follow the prompts. Make sure you use the same Google account each time to access your saved jobs.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Settings â†’ Account â†’ Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

What is LOTO and how do I use it?

LOTO (Lockout/Tagout) is a safety checklist built into the app to help you follow proper isolation procedures before servicing equipment. Access it from within any job record under the Safety tab.

My photos are not uploading. What should I do?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure the app has permission to access your camera and photos in device Settings
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • If the problem persists, contact us below

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen tap Forgot Password and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link shortly. If you signed up with Google, use the Sign in with Google button instead.
